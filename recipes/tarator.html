Tarator


Descrption

This is a bulgarian dish. Its principal ingredients are cucumber, yogurt and garlic

Ingredients

  • 2 cucumbers
  • 500g plain yogurt
  • 4 garlic gloves
  • 3 tablespoons of crushed walnuts
  • A bunch of fresh dill
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Cut the cucumbers into cubes and put them in a bowl.
  2. Beat the yogurt with a fork until it gets liquida and pour it over the cucumbers.
  3. Add crushed garlic, the walnuts and the minced dill as well as salt and oil to tates.
  4. If needed, add some water.
  5. Put it to the refrigerator to cool or add ice cubes.
  6. As revenge, serve cold.
