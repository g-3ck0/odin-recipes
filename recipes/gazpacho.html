Gazpacho

Description

The gazpacho is a traditional spanish dish. It's served cold and it's completely vegan.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pear tomatoe
  • A green pepper
  • A cucumber
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 50 ml olive oil
  • 50g bread
  • 250ml water
  • 5g salt
  • 30ml Jerez vinegar

Steps

  1. Cut all the ingredients.
  2. Put them in the blender and add the olive oil, the water and the vinegar.
  3. 4 mins at max power.
  4. Serve cold.
