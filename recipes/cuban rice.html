Cuban Rice

Description

It's a cuban dish which principals ingredients are rice, tomato sauce, fried banana and egg.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of sofrito
  • 3 tablespoons Spanish virgin olive oil
  • 2 cups of uncooked rice
  • 4 cups of water
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 large eggs

Steps

  1. Make the sofrito and while it's cooking make the rice.
  2. Pour olive oil into a frying pan and fry the eggs. Sprink with a pinch of salt.
  3. Fry the bananas
  4. Place a scoop of rice on each plate and make a depression in the center. Ladle some of the sofrito into the depression. Carefully top with a fried egg and side it whit the banana.
